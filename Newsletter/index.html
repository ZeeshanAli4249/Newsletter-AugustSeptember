NEWSLETTER - 2nd Edition

3ndey Excels at Seamless Middle East & Saudi Arabia Exhibition

3ndey successfully participated in the Seamless Middle East & Saudi Arabia exhibition, held on September 4th and 5th, 2023, at the Riyadh Front Exhibition and Conference Center in Riyadh, Saudi Arabia. Seamless is a premier global platform for fintech, payments, retail, e-commerce, home delivery, and digital marketing, with an annual footfall of over 30,000 attendees. The conference featured insightful presentations, panel discussions, and roundtables from regional and international experts.
3ndey took center stage to exhibit our state-of-the-art, AI-enabled ERP solutions. Our team gave live demonstrations of our game-changing modules, including accounting, procurement, inventory, WMS, CRM, POS, Self-checkout, HR, and Asset Management. We received overwhelmingly positive feedback from attendees, many of whom expressed interest in how our solutions are shaping the future of omnichannel sales in the B2C and B2B spaces.

NEWSLETTER - 2nd Edition

DentalWell: Nurturing Employee Health through Free Consultation Camp

In our commitment to providing a healthy and supportive work environment, the Management organized a complimentary Dental Health Consultation Camp for all employees on Friday, September 1, 2023, from 06:15 P.M. to 08:15 P.M. in the office basement.
This was a great opportunity for everyone to prioritize their dental well-being. Employees had the chance to consult with qualified dentists and receive personalized advice on maintaining good oral health. The dentists also provided free dental checkups and cleanings.
We believe that a healthy workforce is a productive workforce. We are committed to providing our employees with the resources and support they need to stay healthy and happy.
