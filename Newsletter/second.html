Amidst much excitement, our yearly dinner on August 6th, 2023, held at the Luxus Grand Hotel, shines as the highlight of the year. The Mammoth-AI family came together for this special event, celebrating all that we have achieved as a team. It was a heartfelt salute to the effort and hard work of every individual. The peak of the night was the awards ceremony, recognizing those who have stood by the company's growth and the top achievers who went above and beyond. As we look back on this enchanting evening, let's carry its spirit forward, continuing to thrive as one big, joyful Mammoth-AI family.
